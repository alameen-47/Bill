Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Bill47 ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, disclosed, and safeguarded by the Bill47 mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you create an account or use our services, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Account Information: Shop name, email address, and password (encrypted)
  • Business Information: Product details, inventory data, and billing information you enter
  • Transaction Data: Bills, invoices, and receipts you create using the app

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, time spent on the app, and interaction data
  • Location Data: Approximate location for Bluetooth device scanning (Android only)

Permissions We Request

  • Internet: Required for API communication and data synchronization
  • Camera: Used to capture product images for inventory management
  • Media/Photos: Required to select product images from your device
  • Bluetooth: Used to discover and connect to thermal printers for receipt printing
  • Location: Required for Bluetooth device scanning on Android devices

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our billing and invoicing services
  • Process your transactions and generate bills/receipts
  • Connect to Bluetooth thermal printers for receipt printing
  • Authenticate your account and secure access to the app
  • Send you important updates and notifications related to your account
  • Respond to your comments, questions, and provide customer support
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve user experience and app performance

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on our servers with industry-standard encryption. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption of sensitive data (passwords are hashed)
  • Secure API communication using HTTPS
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Access controls to limit employee access to personal data

You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties except in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share data with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our app and conducting our business
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information when required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user data may be transferred

5. Third-Party Services

Our app may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you access.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate personal information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal requirements)
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Withdrawal of Consent: Withdraw consent for processing where consent was the legal basis

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.
